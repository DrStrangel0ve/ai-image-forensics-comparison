## Working Title

When AI Image Detectors Travel: Source-Heldout Diagnostics for Physical, Neural, and Frozen-Encoder Forensics

## One-Sentence Claim

AI-image forensic detectors need source-heldout evaluation because ranking, probability calibration, fake-call rate, and high-confidence triage can disagree sharply when generator source or image processing changes.

## Poster Spine

1. Same-domain accuracy is only the warm-up: `combined_v3` and ResNet-18 tie on Ishu, while physics-guided fusion improves the in-domain anchor.
2. Transfer ranking has a different winner: frozen CLIP is the strongest standalone cross-domain ranker and triage branch.
3. SCP-Fusion is most useful as a diagnostic protocol: it exposes calibration, fake-call-rate, and transform-specific failure modes rather than simply replacing the best branch.
4. Source-aware thresholds and triage produce more defensible forensic decisions than one default threshold everywhere.
